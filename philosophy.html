Explaining why we built VonCMS and why the modern web is broken.

Why VonCMS?

VonCMS started because we were tired of the "Bloat-First" mentality. Most modern CMS solutions fall into two categories: they are either too heavy (WordPress) or too complex (Enterprise Headless).

We are the Pragmatic Rebel. We rebel against the status quo by being practically efficient. We don't want 1,000 plugins to do a simple task. We want 200 core files that actually work.

"We prioritize the publisher, not the plugin developer. If a feature is essential, it should be in the core. If it's not, it shouldn't exist."

The Headless Trap

The "Headless CMS" trend promised freedom but often delivered a higher bill and more complexity. Many headless solutions charge per API call, per user, or per content type.

VonCMS gives you the Headless advantage without the Headless trap:

No Per-API Cost

It's your server. Call your API 1 million times. It costs you $0 extra.

Native React 19

The frontend is a modern Vite project. No complex "connector" needed.

Vendor Lock-in

When you use a proprietary CMS, you are renting your own data. If they raise prices or shut down, you are stuck.

VonCMS is Self-Hosted. You own the database. You own the files. You can move from a $5 VPS to a dedicated server in 5 minutes. No one can pull the plug on your content business.

WordPress Legacy

WordPress is 20+ years of legacy code layered on top of itself. It is the definition of a monolith that has lost its way.

VonCMS is 56x lighter than a standard WordPress install. This isn't just a number—it means faster SEO rankings, lower server CPU usage, and a developer experience that doesn't involve hunting for CSS conflicts in 50 different plugins.

Our Beliefs

We believe the web should be fast, private, and owned by the creators.

Performance is a Human Right

A slow site is a barrier to information. VonCMS is built for sub-second speeds.

Security via Simplicity

Fewer moving parts means fewer attack vectors. 73 API handlers, all audited.
